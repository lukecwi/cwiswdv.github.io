Our Story:

Our story is one from field to bowl. Our founder Brian Rakenton was born in the early 30s and the great depression was just underway. In Iowa, Brian was surrounded by corn and fell in love with it. He spent his early years working in the fields and at the age of 18 went to The University of Iowa. He returned to his father's fields and took over the farm, turning it into the first pop magic farm.
