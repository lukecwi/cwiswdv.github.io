From humble origins. To a mightly present.

Our founder, Martin Chujak was born in Belgrade, Yugoslavia, in 1889. Martin loved cooking, and when he turned 18 he left Yugoslavia to travel the world. On his travels, he met his mentor in New York and learned the art of noodle-making. After 10 years of being a noodle maker, market left new York and moved to Boise, where he founded noodles da Boise, later renamed Boise Noodles.
